Going Solo: Designing a Seamless Experience for Connecting Solo Travelers

About Going Solo

The Going Solo app is a digital platform designed to help solo travelers find travel buddies who share similar travel interests and destinations. The primary goal is to enhance the solo travel experience by providing a safe and convenient way for travelers to connect with others who might be on the same journey or looking for companionship during their travels.

The Problem

Despite the growing popularity of solo travel, many travelers still face anxiety over safety, language barriers, and loneliness. Finding a compatible travel companion is particularly challenging when friends are unavailable, or when you're new to a city and lack a social circle. There is a need for an easy way to connect with like-minded individuals for shared travel experiences, ensuring safety, companionship, and a more enriching journey.

UX Research

I wanted to collect extensive data for comprehensive research. I explored various websites and articles to understand the tourism landscape and identify potential solutions.

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed design patterns from applications solving similar problems, including Meetup, Tripr, Travello, and Couchsurfing to identify common patterns and user expectations.

Key Finding 1

Most existing apps focus on group activities rather than one-on-one travel buddy matching.

Key Finding 2

Safety and trust are common concerns, with many apps lacking robust verification features.

Key Finding 3

Users appreciate filtering companions by interests, location, and travel dates.
