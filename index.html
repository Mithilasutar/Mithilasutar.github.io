Going Solo: Designing a Seamless Experience for Connecting Solo Travelers

About Going Solo

The Going Solo app is a digital platform designed to help solo travelers find travel buddies who share similar travel interests and destinations. The primary goal is to enhance the solo travel experience by providing a safe and convenient way for travelers to connect with others who might be on the same journey or looking for companionship during their travels.

The Problem

Despite the growing popularity of solo travel, many travelers still face anxiety over safety, language barriers, and loneliness. Finding a compatible travel companion is particularly challenging when friends are unavailable, or when you're new to a city and lack a social circle. There is a need for an easy way to connect with like-minded individuals for shared travel experiences, ensuring safety, companionship, and a more enriching journey.

User Pain Points

Through analyzing user reviews of similar apps, I identified critical pain points:

⚠️

Safety Concerns

Users express concerns about meeting strangers and demand robust safety features.

👥

Low User Base

Difficulty finding travel companions, especially in less popular destinations.

🐛

Technical Issues

Apps suffer from bugs, crashes, and navigation difficulties.

Target Audience

🧳

Solo Travelers

Individuals who enjoy traveling alone but are open to meeting new people and sharing parts of their journey.

🗺️

New Travelers

Those who are new to traveling solo and might feel more comfortable having a companion for certain parts of their trip.

⚡

Adventurous Spirits

People who love spontaneous travel and are looking for like-minded individuals to explore with.

Onboarding & Sign Up

The onboarding screen provides users with a friendly vibe and highlights the key advantages the app delivers. The primary registration method is email with OTP verification.

Navigation Structure

I classified the app's functionality into five primary categories shown in the bottom tab bar:

Explore Quick overview and personalized recommendations
Feed Discover travel buddies, events, and destinations
Trips Manage and organize travel plans and itineraries
Profile Edit settings, view travel journal, manage account
Messages Communicate with potential travel companions

Home Screen & Nearby Features

The home page shows solo travelers within 100km near you, groups near you, and live hangouts. Users can create their own groups that others can freely join.

Feed 🔥

The Feed tab shows trending content including travel images, posts looking for travel buddies, trip itineraries, events, and forum discussions.

Travel Buddy Matching

A premium swipe-based feature ($3.99) where users can match with potential travel buddies based on shared interests, destinations, and various factors.
